But what exactly are essential oils? For
those new to this trend, these incredibly
potent oils — some of which have been
around for centuries — are extracted from
plants to capture their flavors, scents, and
overall beneficial properties.

An essential oil is a concentrated
hydrophobic liquid containing volatile
(easily evaporated at normal
temperatures) chemical compounds from
plants. Essential oils are also known as
volatile oils, ethereal oils, aetherolea, or
simply as the oil of the plant from which
they were extracted, such as oil of clove.
